
------------------------------------------TIC TAC TOE GAME-------------------------------------------------------


After executing the game, follow the next steps:

1. Choose the mode:

   To choose the mode 1: Player vs Player, introduce the number 1.
   To choose the mode 2: Player vs AI, introduce the number 2.

2. Choose the color of each player. Introduce rojo for red color and amarillo for yellow.

3. Choose in which column you want to put your token.

4. Keep playing.
